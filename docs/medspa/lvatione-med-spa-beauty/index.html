01 — What's Working

A 4.7-star rating across 772 reviews combined with an active Instagram presence shows that Elevatione has built real community trust in West Palm Beach. The brand's bilingual and inclusive positioning is a differentiator in a diverse South Florida market, and that community goodwill is worth significantly more when paired with an automated system to keep those clients returning.

02 — Missed Bookings Without Follow-Up

Visitors who discover Elevatione through Instagram or Google and leave without booking are not entered into any automated follow-up sequence, so each unconverted visit is a permanently lost lead. In a visually driven market like West Palm Beach where clients are constantly discovering new options on social media, the practices that follow up automatically after initial interest convert dramatically more leads than those that wait for clients to return on their own.

03 — Inactive Client Leakage

With 772 reviews representing a large base of past clients, there is a meaningful volume of people who visited once or twice and have since gone quiet. Without an automated reactivation system, these clients drift to newer or more aggressive competitors simply because no one reached out. A targeted SMS campaign to clients who have not visited in 60 to 90 days is consistently one of the highest-return actions for a practice of this size.

04 — No Automated Review Generation

Seven hundred and seventy-two reviews is a strong foundation but maintaining competitive visibility in West Palm Beach requires a steady flow of new reviews rather than a large historical count. Without an automated post-visit review request, the practice's review velocity is unpredictable and depends on client initiative alone. A consistent automated request sent within 24 hours of every visit keeps the review count growing week over week.

05 — No Visible Membership or Loyalty System

There is no membership, loyalty program, or prepaid package structure visible in the current client experience, which means client retention depends entirely on the client's own initiative to rebook. Practices that introduce a simple monthly membership for recurring treatments see measurably higher booking frequency and a stronger emotional connection between clients and the brand. This is especially relevant for a community-focused brand like Elevatione.
