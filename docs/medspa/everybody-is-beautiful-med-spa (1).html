01 — What's Working

Your reputation is genuinely excellent. A 4.9-star rating across 107 Google reviews tells us your clients love the experience once they are in the door. The problem is not the service, it is that most of those happy clients are never systematically brought back or asked to refer a friend.

02 — Missed Bookings

Every week, potential clients visit your website or Instagram, do not book, and disappear with no way to follow up. There is no SMS or email capture on your site, so once someone leaves without booking, they are gone for good. No automated follow-up means no second chance, and in a competitive Boca Raton market that is a significant amount of lost revenue every month.

03 — Inactive Client Leakage

Med spa clients who do not hear from a business within 60 to 90 days typically move on to a competitor, not because they were unhappy but simply because they forgot. With 107 reviews worth of past clients, Everybody Is Beautiful has a valuable database that is almost certainly going cold without an automated reactivation system to bring them back. A single reactivation campaign targeting clients who have not visited in 90 days can generate thousands of dollars in appointments in a matter of days.

04 — No Review Generation System

Your 4.9-star average is exceptional but 107 reviews is a relatively low volume for a med spa that has clearly been in business long enough to build that reputation. Without an automated post-visit SMS asking happy clients to leave a review, you are leaving your Google ranking lower than it should be. More reviews mean higher placement in local searches, which means more new clients finding you organically without spending anything on ads.

05 — Retention Revenue Gap

There is no visible membership, package bundle, or loyalty program on your website or in your booking flow. Med spas that implement even a basic membership or prepaid package system retain clients at a significantly higher rate and create predictable monthly revenue. Right now every client relationship starts from zero each visit, with no automated touchpoint keeping them engaged between appointments.

06 — Quick Wins
1
Launch a 90-day reactivation campaign Send an automated SMS to every past client who has not booked in 90 days with a simple offer to come back. For a practice with your review volume, this alone can generate 10 to 20 booked appointments in the first week without spending a dollar on ads.
2
Automate post-visit review requests Set up an SMS that goes out automatically 24 hours after every appointment asking the client to leave a Google review. Doubling your review count from 107 to 200 plus would meaningfully improve your position in Boca Raton local search results.
3
Add SMS capture to your booking flow Collect phone numbers from every visitor who does not book so you can follow up automatically with a short sequence. Most clients need two to three touches before they commit, and right now those leads are disappearing with no system to catch them.
